Magnolia Bakery - Bangalore

Magnolia Bakery is a delightful destination in Bangalore, known for its scrumptious desserts and charming ambiance. One of their most famous treats is the Milk Cake, which went viral after being praised by Bollywood celebrities!

Popular Food Items at Magnolia Bakery

Signature Desserts:

  1. Classic Milk Cake
  2. Banana Pudding
  3. Red Velvet Cupcake
  4. Cheesecake
  5. Chocolate Fudge Brownie

Other Delicacies:

  • Blueberry Muffins
  • Carrot Cake
  • Cookies and Cream Cupcake
  • Lemon Bars
  • Peanut Butter Cookies

Why Visit Magnolia Bakery?

If you have a sweet tooth, this bakery is a paradise! The cozy atmosphere, fresh ingredients, and mouthwatering desserts make it a must-visit spot in Bangalore.
